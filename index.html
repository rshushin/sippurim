SupplySignal→

Supply chain risk intelligence – monitors global news and disaster feeds to identify threats to a customer's supplier network. Operators see where their suppliers are exposed to geopolitical events, severe weather, conflict, port closures, and disease outbreaks. Each supplier gets a live risk score and a dossier covering financial, ESG, geopolitical, and dependency exposure. When a threat hits the network, the team receives an email digest with affected suppliers ranked by impact – no more manual news monitoring or scrambling spreadsheets. Continuous visibility into supplier risk that replaces ad-hoc tracking.
